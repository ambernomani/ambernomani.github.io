The Company

ULTIMATE Animation Studio is a UAE based animation studio that creates animated feature films, short films and TV commercials etc. It was founded on May 5th, 2018, by three star students from New York University Abu Dhabi.

We create:

  • Full length animated feature films
  • Virtual reality films
  • Animated architectural visualizations
  • University student life advertisement

But no matter the subject, our mission is always to create the best experience for the users, to deliver engaging stories with compelling characters and the utmost quality.
